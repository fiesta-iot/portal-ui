What is FIESTA-IoT ?

FIESTA-IoT is project that provides a Blueprint Experimental Infrastructure for Heterogeneous IoT Technologies. FIESTA-IoT provides tools, techniques, processes and best practices enabling IoT testbed/platforms operators to interconnect their facilities in an interoperable way based upon cutting edge semantics-based solutions.

Testbeds & Experiments

The FIESTA-IoT platform federates 10 IoT testbeds providing access to a wealth of heterogeneous IoT data. These ten testbeds are geographically distributed, and have distinct data offerings that can be combined to develop innovative IoT experiments.
